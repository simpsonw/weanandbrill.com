The Windmill Inn

Most of the wedding party will be staying at this cozy hotel in St. Phillip's Plaza, just a stone's throw away from our apartment. Rooms typically go for somewhere around $100 a night.

The Windmill Inn Website

The Westward Look

If you're looking for a first class resort experience in Tucson, this is the place to be. Rooms generally go for $170-$200 and up.

The Westward Look Website

Hotel Congress

If you're looking for a unique stay, our wedding venue, Hotel Congress, offers room with classic 1930s-style ambience. However, please know that Hotel Congress has an all night club in it and a train yard nearby, so this is not the place to go if you're looking for peace and quiet. Rooms generally range from between $89-149 per night.
