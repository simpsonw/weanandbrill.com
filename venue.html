Venue

Hotel Congress

About 30 seconds into our tour of Hotel Congress, we knew that we wanted to use it as our wedding venue. This historic hotel was built in 1919 and has been a Tucson landmark ever since. Its elegant Art Deco stylings and quirky charm have made it a hit with visitors from all over, including the infamous bank robber John Dillinger, who was captured at the hotel on January 22nd, 1934.

Our wedding will be in the beautiful Copper Hall in the hotel. As the name suggests, the walls are covered with copper leaf, giving the room a pleasant, warm glow.

Location

The hotel is located at 311 E. Congress Street, Tucson, Arizona 85701. Parking is available just east of the hotel in the Plaza Centro Garage at 345 E. Congress.
